how many members are in the stray kids?

Kids is composed of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin,
Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. (There was originally a ninth member
of the group, Woojin, but he left Stray Kids in 2019 for undisclosed personal reasons.)

How did Stray Kids get their start?

Stray Kids has a very specific origin story: The group’s eight members were originally
brought together through a South Korean reality survival show, also called Stray Kids,
that aired in 2017. It followed the band’s members as they attempted to rise through
the ranks of the now-saturated K-pop industry. They have come a long way since then; in
2023, they won the Best K-Pop Award at the MTV Music Video Awards, propelled in part by
their avid fanbase, that goes by the name “Stay.”

How many albums does Stray Kids have out?

Stray Kids has released four studio albums (three Korean and one Japanese), four compilation albums, one reissue, 13 extended plays (10 Korean and three Japanese), two single albums, and thirty singles…all since officially forming a mere seven years ago. Generally, they are regarded as having a grungier, more rock-influenced sound than some of their K-Pop contemporaries.
